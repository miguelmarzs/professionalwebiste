Tesla Consulting Project

This paper aims to tackle those challenges by recommending two business strategies and one corporate-level strategy. To identify these strategies, an external analysis using five porters forces was performed. A high risk for bargain power of suppliers was identified. Additionally, using a VRIN chart to analyze the internal environment of Tesla, we discovered that brand recognition and innovative products were among the top sustainable competitive advantages for Tesla. A financial analysis was also conducted to assess the financial health of the company. This financial analysis resulted in understanding the high growth rate of the company and the need for capital to backup such growth.
